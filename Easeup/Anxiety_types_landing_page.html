Understanding Anxiety

Explore the different types of anxiety disorders, their symptoms, and find the support you need on your mental health journey.

40M Adults Affected
10 Main Types
100% Treatable
😌

Normal Anxiety

Natural Response

A natural emotional response to stress or uncertainty that everyone experiences from time to time.

Common Signs:
Temporary worry, nervousness before events, manageable stress responses
🌪️

Generalized Anxiety Disorder

Persistent Worry

Excessive, uncontrollable worry about everyday situations that persists for months.

Key Symptoms:
Restlessness, fatigue, difficulty concentrating, muscle tension
⚡

Panic Disorder

Intense Episodes

Recurrent, unexpected panic attacks with intense fear and physical symptoms.

Panic Symptoms:
Heart palpitations, sweating, trembling, shortness of breath
👥

Social Anxiety Disorder

Social Fear

Intense fear of social situations and being judged or embarrassed by others.

Social Signs:
Blushing, excessive sweating, avoiding social events, fear of speaking
🕷️

Specific Phobias

Object/Situation Fear

Excessive and irrational fear of specific objects, animals, or situations.

Common Phobias:
Heights, spiders, flying, blood, enclosed spaces, animals
🏠

Agoraphobia

Space/Escape Fear

Fear of places or situations where escape might be difficult or help unavailable.

Avoided Places:
Crowds, public transport, open spaces, being alone outside home
👶

Separation Anxiety Disorder

Attachment Fear

Excessive fear of being separated from important people or places of security.

Signs Include:
Clinginess, nightmares, refusal to leave home, physical complaints
🤐

Selective Mutism

Communication Anxiety

Inability to speak in specific social situations despite speaking normally elsewhere.

Characteristics:
Silence in school/public, normal speech at home, social withdrawal
🔄

Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)

Repetitive Patterns

Unwanted intrusive thoughts (obsessions) and repetitive behaviors (compulsions).

Common Patterns:
Hand washing, checking, counting, arranging, intrusive thoughts
💭

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder

Trauma Response

Anxiety disorder that develops after experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event.

Core Symptoms:
Flashbacks, nightmares, avoidance, emotional numbing, hypervigilance

Need Professional Help?

If you're experiencing symptoms of anxiety that interfere with your daily life, remember that help is available. Professional treatment can significantly improve your quality of life.
