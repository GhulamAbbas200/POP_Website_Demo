We are a group of university students passionate about mental health advocacy. Our mission is to spread awareness about anxiety and help remove the stigma surrounding mental health in our society. Through Easeup, we aim to provide accessible, relatable, and practical information to help individuals understand anxiety, recognize its impact, and explore healthy ways to cope.
